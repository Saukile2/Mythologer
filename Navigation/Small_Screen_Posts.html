About Mythologer

How We Started

Mythologer Website started in 2020 as intellectual computer tutors website, then later in 2021 the developer behind decided to focus on something new hence changing the website to mythologer so it only focuses on rendering information to individuals who seek facts.

Our Main Purpose

We are currently focused on exploring stories considered as myths, or untrue or legends, we focus on those stories that can change an individual's view of things!, we believe Knowledge is power so we filter out all unnecessary chunks of a story before posting it here to make sure that we give you the best information possible.

Who Can Contribute

Anyone can contribute to our stories and posts by sending us an email using the info provided below, we will verify, edit, and reconstruct any stories you contribute via email, before posting here to avoid confusion and misleading people, send your contributions through email to Saukileb@gmail.com

Request Info

You can ask for any story to be poste here, and we will possibly post within days, but in some cases, we may not be able to grant your request in which case we will contact you to let you know why we failed to grant your request! by sending email to Saukileb@gmail.com
